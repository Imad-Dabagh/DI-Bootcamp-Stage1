Who is Elon Musk?

Elon Musk is a billionaire entrepreneur, inventor, and CEO of multiple groundbreaking companies, including SpaceX, Tesla, Neuralink, and The Boring Company. Renowned for his ambitious vision, he aims to revolutionize transportation with electric vehicles and autonomous driving, reshape space exploration through reusable rockets and plans for Mars colonization, and advance neural technology to enhance human-computer interaction. Musk has also championed renewable energy solutions, expanding global access to solar power and battery storage systems through Tesla Energy.

Timeline of Elon Musk's Life

  • 1971: Born in Pretoria, South Africa.
  • 1995: Co-founded Zip2, a city guide software for newspapers.
  • 1999: Sold Zip2 and co-founded X.com, which later became PayPal.
  • 2002: Founded SpaceX with the goal of reducing space transportation costs.
  • 2004: Joined Tesla Motors, Inc. (now Tesla, Inc.) as chairman and product architect.
  • 2008: SpaceX's Falcon 1 became the first privately-funded liquid-fueled rocket to reach orbit.
  • 2015: Co-founded OpenAI, a non-profit AI research company.
  • 2020: Became the world's richest person.
